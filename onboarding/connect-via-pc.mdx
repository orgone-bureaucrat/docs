## Overview

Kisuke Connect creates a secure tunnel between your phone and computer, allowing you to:

- Access terminal sessions on your PC from your phone
- Edit files on your computer remotely
- Run commands on your development machine

## Prerequisites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Kisuke on your phone">
    Download from [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/kisuke) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.kisuke)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Kisuke Connect on your PC">
    - [Mac](/installation/mac)
    - [Windows](/installation/windows)
    - [Linux](/installation/linux)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to both">
    Use the same account (Claude, Google, or OpenAI) on both devices
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connection Setup

### On Your Computer

1. Open Kisuke Connect
2. Ensure you're signed in
3. Check the status shows "Connected"

### On Your Phone

1. Open Kisuke
2. Tap the **Connect** tab
3. Your computer should appear under **Devices**
4. Tap your computer to connect

<Check>
Once connected, you'll see a green indicator next to your computer's name.
</Check>

## Using the Connection

### Terminal Access

1. In Kisuke, tap **Terminal**
2. Select your connected computer
3. You now have shell access to your PC

### File Editor

1. Tap **Editor**
2. Browse files on your connected computer
3. Open and edit files directly

### AI Chat with Context

When connected, AI chat can:

- Access files on your computer
- Run commands with your permission
- Help debug code in your projects

## Troubleshooting

### Devices Not Appearing

- Ensure both devices are signed into the same account
- Check that Kisuke Connect is running on your PC
- Verify your PC has internet connectivity

### Connection Drops

- Check your network stability
- The connection will automatically reconnect
- Try restarting Kisuke Connect on your PC

### Slow Connection

If direct connection fails, Kisuke falls back to relay servers. For best performance:

- Ensure both devices can establish UDP connections
- Open port 41641 if behind a restrictive firewall
