## Overview

Kisuke Connect is a desktop companion app that allows your mobile Kisuke app to connect to your Mac. It works similarly to Tailscale, creating a secure connection between your devices.

## Installation

### Download

Download Kisuke Connect for Mac from our website or use Homebrew:

```bash
brew install kisuke-connect
```

Or download directly:

<Card title="Download for Mac" icon="apple" href="https://kisuke.app/download/mac">
  Kisuke Connect for macOS (Universal)
</Card>

### System Requirements

- macOS 12 (Monterey) or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac

## Setup

1. **Install** Kisuke Connect
2. **Open** the app from Applications
3. **Sign in** with the same account you use on your mobile device
4. **Approve** the connection when prompted on your phone

## Connecting Your Devices

Once Kisuke Connect is running on your Mac:

1. Open Kisuke on your mobile device
2. Go to **Settings** > **Connections**
3. Your Mac should appear in the list
4. Tap to connect

<Note>
Both devices must be signed in with the same account.
</Note>
